Interview with İlhanur KARACA

1) Can you give me a short information about your biofeedback/background?
— I graduated from Ereğli High School and then Hacettepe University. I was not a hardworking student but I used to study... especially thanks to this method I could enter the university in my first year easily.

2) Was it this job which you were dreaming about?
— I wanted to be a pharmacist, a dentist or a mathematician but I wanted to be the best of my area whatever my job is.

3) Are you satisfied with your job?
— Yes, I am working with a great pleasure because it is very important to be helpful, especially in such a situation. If you can give a nice feeling which you can experience when witnessing a disabled person who can walk after all your treatment.

4) Do you advise us to choose this job?
— I really willingly recommend it certainly. If you do this job... As I said it is a difficult job because you are face to face with the disabled and the old. You should be real volunteers.

5) Why did you choose this job?
— My brothers led me to this job.

6) What was your first work area?
— After graduation, I worked in Ankara for 4 years.
