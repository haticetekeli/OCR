--- DIALOGUE 1: WRONG TELEPHONE MANNERS ---
Secretary: Hello I am Sema. Who are you?
Müşteri: This is Ahmet the Engineer.
Müşteri: I would like to speak to Mr. Şevket Yılmaz.
Secretary: He is outside, call him later.
Müşteri: What time can I call back?
Secretary: I don't know.
Müşteri: You are very rude. Goodbye!

--- DIALOGUE 2: CORRECT TELEPHONE MANNERS ---
Secretary: Good morning, Somuncu company. How can I help you?
Müşteri: This is Mr. Mücahit Okçu. I want to speak to Mr. Hakan Şam.
Secretary: Mr. Hakan Şam. Just a moment, let me connect.
Müşteri: Thank you. Goodbye.

--- DIALOGUE 3: ANOTHER EXAMPLE (MEETING DIALOGUE) ---
Müşteri: This is Mr. Bülent. I would like to speak to Mr. Sinan Cebeci.
Secretary: Mr. Cebeci is at the meeting now, I don't know what time he will be back. Should I ask him to call you?
Müşteri: Okay, goodbye.
